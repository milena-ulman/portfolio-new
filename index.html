About me

UX/UI Desinger & Web developer

Hi, my name is Milena.

I started my adventure with creating websites less than a year ago.

Initially, I wanted to be associated with the front-end world and that's why I learned HTML, CSS and to a small extent JS with jQuery.

During development I discovered that UX and UI is by far more interesting to me. Creating unique designs and participating in new projects always excites me!

Why would you choose me?

I love exploring new ways to absorb knowledge more effectively.

What drives me the most is passion for my work.

I enjoy new challenges, there is some excitement behind them.

You bring the idea - I listen, I care..

While designing new products, I aim for unleashing my creative spirit.

Empathy is important to me, so is kindness :).

How I work

Research

Looking for similar websites or applications, drawing based on inspiration from the other projects.

Prototype

Creating a prototype (mock-up). Choosing colors, fonts, graphics. Composing up a visual guideline of the project.

Demo & Feedback

Presenting first design version to the client. Applying design changes based on feedback. Increment and improve.

Implementation

After client approves final design, I am picking up appropriate technologies and starting development.
